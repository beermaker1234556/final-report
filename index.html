The Anatomy of Our Irish Stout

The Recipe

Our authentic Irish Stout is crafted from a simple, high-quality recipe designed for a rich, smooth, and roasty flavor profile. The key is balance.

🌾
Irish Pale & Flaked Malt: The foundation for fermentable sugars and a creamy, smooth body.
🔥
Roasted Barley: The soul of the stout, providing the signature dark color and notes of coffee and chocolate.
🌿
Magnum & Golding Hops: A dual-hop charge for clean bitterness and a subtle, earthy aroma.
🔬
Irish Ale Yeast: A specific yeast strain that ferments cleanly, allowing the rich malt character to dominate.

The Brewing Process

1. Mashing

Grains are steeped in hot water to convert starches into fermentable sugars.

2. Boiling & Hopping

The sugary liquid ("wort") is boiled while hops are added for bitterness and aroma.

3. Fermentation

Yeast is added to the cooled wort to convert sugars into alcohol and CO2.

4. Maturation & Packaging

The beer is conditioned to mellow flavors before being canned for distribution.
